Statistical Analysis of Patient-Centric HIE among Respondents to 2015 AHA Annual Survey ITS, Mar’18 – April’18

Assessment of patient engagement functionalities offered by hospitals across the US per the survey, with a focus on features offered to patients to access and manage their health records and the mechanisms available for doing so. Some key findings: ● Government and non-profit hospitals tend to offer their patients more features for managing their healthcare information. ● The extent to which a hospital shared information with other hospitals & clinics within and outside its network had a significant bearing on the extent of facilities offered.
